Displays the frequencies of equal-tempered intercals for N notes per octave.

If N is 12, we will get the notes of the standard chromatic scale. Another polular division is 19 notes.

Usage: nscale notes midinote

Notes: how many steps it takes for the full range

Midinote: starting midinote

Interval: 2.0 is one octave, 4.0 is two octaves and 1.5 is half an octave
